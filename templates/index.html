Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Mental Survey work?

The Mental Survey is a personalized assessment tool designed to gauge your current mental well-being. Answer a series of questions, and we'll provide insights into your stress and anxiety levels to help guide your mental health journey.

What kind of advice can I expect from the chat bot?

Our chat bot offers supportive and thoughtful advice, emulating the understanding of a friend. Whether you need someone to listen, provide coping strategies, or offer encouragement, our virtual companion is here for you.

How does the emotion recognition tool detect smiles?

The emotion recognition tool uses advanced technology to analyze facial expressions, specifically detecting smiles. When you express joy, the app acknowledges and celebrates those positive moments, contributing to a supportive and uplifting environment.

Is Calmy suitable for all ages?

While Calmy is designed to support a wide range of users, it's essential to consider individual needs. If you have specific concerns or questions about the app's suitability, we recommend consulting with a healthcare professional.
